Adam Fidel

I am a quantitative software developer at Quantlab. Previously, I completed my PhD in Computer Science at Texas A&M University where I conducted research in the Parasol Lab focusing on high-performance computing, parallel algorithms and parallel graph processing. During my PhD, I interned at Google Search developing parallel graph algorithms for massive scale graphs.

I worked on the STAPL project and I was the principal developer of the STAPL Graph Library.
