Biography

I am a PhD candidate in computer science in the Parasol Lab at Texas A&M University. My interests are in high-performance computing and parallel graph processing. Specifically, I work on accelerating large-scale parallel graph workloads through the use of asynchronous processing and nested parallelism.

I work on the STAPL project and I am the principal developer of the STAPL Graph Library. My PhD advisors are professors Nancy Amato and Lawrence Rauchwerger.
